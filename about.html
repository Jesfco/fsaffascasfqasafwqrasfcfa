How We Came Together

Tacism Studios came together organically from the ULSS community, where members were passionate about expanding the variety of immersive LSPDFR callouts. The demand sparked the formation of a dev team when sols, an early contributor, took the lead in organizing the project. Shortly after, Dr. Mamba joined the effort, followed by Maminka, a well-known figure in the community for providing tech support. The team grew stronger, and after a few collaborative development cycles, sols stepped down as lead developer. Dr. Mamba assumed the leadership role, with Maminka stepping into an executive management position to keep operations smooth and focused. With the release of their first callout pack, interest in the community grew quickly. That success drew the attention of Demon Hunter, another talented developer, who was brought onto the team after a careful review of their work. Since then, Tacism Studios has continued expanding with new ideas, improved stability, and a shared goal of raising the standard for immersive law enforcement gameplay in GTA V.
